Who I Am

A bubbly, eccentric, and passionate communicator with a deep love of sharing information. Effective and informational communications has always been a driving factor in everything I do. I love to try new things, take challenges in stride, and build community everywhere I go.

Social Media Content

In my current role as a Social Media Advisor at Health Canada, my work focuses on creating accessible, engaging content that helps Canadians understand important public health topics while aligning with the Government of Canada’s health communication priorities.

I collaborate closely with program experts, scientists, and policy teams to translate complex health information into clear and engaging social media content. This includes producing platform-specific posts such as carousels, short-form videos, and educational graphics that make technical information more approachable for the public.

By tracking engagement metrics and audience behaviour, I help identify opportunities to refine messaging and increase the reach and impact of our content. Content is developed both in response to emerging trends and through planned campaigns outlined in our annual editorial calendar. This allows our team to balance timely, relevant content with strategic public health initiatives.
